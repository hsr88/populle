# Canva Prompt - Open Graph Image dla Populle

## Wymiary
**1200 x 630 px** (standardowy format OG)

---

## Prompt (wklej do Canva AI / Magic Media)

```
Create a stunning, cinematic visualization for a world population website. 

Background: Deep space with subtle nebula in dark blue (#0f172a) and cyan (#0ea5e9) tones. 

Center: A glowing 3D globe of Earth showing continents with illuminated city lights at night. The globe should have a subtle cyan glow aura around it. 

Foreground elements: 
- Floating geometric particles representing population data points
- Thin glowing lines connecting major cities across the globe
- Small holographic human silhouettes floating upward around the globe

Text area (leave space at bottom): Clean dark gradient overlay at the bottom third

Color palette: Dark navy, cyan (#0ea5e9), orange accent (#f59e0b), white glows

Style: Futuristic, minimalist, tech-forward, data visualization aesthetic

Mood: Epic, inspiring, scientific, modern
```

---

## Alternatywny (krótszy) prompt:

```
Dark futuristic banner with glowing 3D Earth globe at center, cyan and blue color scheme, floating data particles and connection lines, space nebula background, minimalist tech style, cinematic lighting
```

---

## Elementy do dodania w Canva (tekst):

**Tytuł:**
- Font: Bold sans-serif (np. Inter, Space Grotesk)
- Text: "POPULLE"
- Size: 72-96px
- Color: White with subtle cyan glow

**Subtytuł:**
- Text: "World Population Visualization and Charts"
- Size: 24-32px
- Color: Muted cyan/gray

**Dekoracja:**
- Mała ikona globu lub wykresu w rogu
- Subtelne linie siatki w tle
